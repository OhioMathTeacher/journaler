Journaler · TCE 318P

A quiet place to read your course articles closely and keep your Reading Journal all semester.

  1. Get the reading. Open that week's Reading Homework sheet, follow its JSTOR link, and download the article PDF through Miami's library.
  2. Open it here. Click Open article, choose the PDF, and it appears on the left to read.
  3. Think in the margins. Select any passage to ask Socrates a question; your notes save beside the text.
  4. Write your entry. Open your Notebook and write that week's dated journal entry, using the prompt on the Reading Homework sheet.
  5. Turn it in. At the end of Act I, open the Notebook and choose Print / PDF to submit your whole journal as one file.

On a shared or public computer? This browser may forget your work when you log out. Keep your work in a file — thumb drive, cloud, or email: save it with the ⤓ button (top bar) as you go, and open it here each time. On your own device, your work is remembered automatically. Nothing is ever uploaded.
